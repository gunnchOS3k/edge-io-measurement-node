# Abstract

Privacy-Preserving Edge Measurement Nodes for Community-Scale 6G Digital Twins presents a **research prototype** artifact for community-scale 6G research. Contribution focus: telemetry schema, consent lifecycle, synthetic demo, 7GC export.
Results reported here are from **preliminary toy demonstrations** using synthetic data only; field validation is future work.
We do not claim operational 6G deployment, Oulu/CWC affiliation, or carrier-grade performance.
